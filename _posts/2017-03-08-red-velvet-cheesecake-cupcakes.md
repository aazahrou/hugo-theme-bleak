## I- Ingredients for 24 Cupcakes:

### 1- Cake:
* 1 box Velvet Cake Mix (<a href="https://amzn.to/2miiIDP">Red VelvetÂ Cake on Amazon</a>);
* 6 oz container of Vanilla Greek Yogurt (<a href="https://amzn.to/2n5F9vR">Nonfat Greek Yogurt on Amazon</a>);
* 1 egg;
* 1 cup of water.

### 2- Cream Cheese Frosting:
* 1 block of 8 oz Reduced Fat Cream Cheese (<a href="https://amzn.to/2lXADOu">Best Cream Cheese On Amazon</a>);
* 1/4 cup nonfat Vanilla Greek Yogurt;
* 1/4 cup Light Sour Cream;
* 2 large egg whites;
* 1/4 cup Sugar;
* 1 tbsp Flour;
* 1 tsp Vanilla.

## II- Directions:
1. Pre-Heat oven to 350 F degrees (177 C).
1. Prepare muffin/cupcake tins with foil liners (24); set aside.
1. In a large mixing bowl: beat all the Cake Ingredients on medium speed until all well combined; set aside.
1. In a medium mixing bowl: beat all the Cheesecake Ingredients on med/low until all well combined.
1. Add Red Velvet Cake Mix to the all the cupcake liners, about 1/2 full.
1. Add an ice cream scoop size of the cheesecake mixture on top of the cake mix.
1. Swirl with a fork to combine slightly and get a swirled design (look at picture below).
1. Bake for 25 minutes until the cheesecake is set.
1. Cook and let it cool.
1. Refrigerate in a covered container for 1 hour (minimum). And Enjoy!!
